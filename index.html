Al-Aleem College mathematics club is a learning hub that embraced students of the college from different classes, different departments and different instructors coming together as one for the sole purpose of helping the college achieve its aims, goals and pre-set objectives. We are not gender biased neither are we age discriminating. We bring together unity amongst culture and religion and we are not known for racism. We are Mathematicians, we are Technologists, we are Developers, we are Programmers and of course we are PROBLEM SOLVERS!.
